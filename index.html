About TOS Hub

At TOS Hub (The Other Side), we are building the foundation of Nigeria's Silicon Valley — a space where ideas are transformed into real businesses, where entrepreneurs are supported to solve problems with technology, and where opportunity is accessible to all.

Our mission is to remove barriers and create pathways — connecting innovators with the tools, mentorship, capital, and networks they need to succeed.

Our Mission

Our mission is to catalyze Africa's transformation by equipping its youth with the power to invent, build, and lead in a digital-first world.
