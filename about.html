About Us

We don't just dream of change — we build it.

At TOS Hub (The Other Side), we are building the foundation of Nigeria's Silicon Valley — a space where ideas are transformed into real businesses, where entrepreneurs are supported to solve problems with technology, and where opportunity is accessible to all.

Nigeria is full of brilliant, resilient, and creative people. What often holds us back is not talent, but opportunity. At TOS Hub, our mission is to remove barriers and create pathways — connecting innovators with the tools, mentorship, capital, and networks they need to succeed.

Our Vision

To unlock Nigeria's immense potential by creating an ecosystem where entrepreneurs don't have to leave home to succeed — they can build world-class solutions right here.

Our Approach

  • Incubate: Provide practical support to shape and test ideas.
  • Connect: Link founders with global networks and diaspora expertise.
  • Fund: Offer early-stage investment and access to capital.
  • Scale: Build companies with real traction, designed for growth.

Why TOS Hub Matters

  • Nigeria is Africa's largest, youngest market — full of opportunities in fintech, health, education, agriculture, and logistics.
  • By bridging local innovators with diaspora professionals and global investors, we create a unique ecosystem others cannot replicate.
  • We balance impact and return — building businesses that change lives and create sustainable economic value.

At TOS Hub, we are shaping The Other Side of Nigeria — the side of innovation, resilience, and global relevance.
