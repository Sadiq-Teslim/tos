About Us

We don’t just dream of change — we build it.

Building the Foundation of Nigeria’s Silicon Valley.

At TOS Hub (The Other Side), our mission is to remove barriers and create pathways for Africa's brightest minds. We connect innovators with the tools, mentorship, capital, and networks they need to transform brilliant ideas into world-class businesses.

Nigeria is full of resilient and creative talent. We believe that by fostering a supportive ecosystem, we can unlock this immense potential and build the future, right here at home.

Our Core Pillars

Incubate & Innovate

We provide the practical support, workspace, and expert guidance needed to shape, test, and launch groundbreaking ideas.

Connect & Collaborate

We bridge the gap between local founders, diaspora professionals, and global investors, creating a powerful and diverse support network.

Fund & Fuel Growth

Through early-stage investment and access to capital, we provide the critical fuel required to scale companies designed for real-world impact.

Why TOS Hub Matters

Africa's Largest Market

Nigeria is the continent's largest, youngest market—full of opportunities in fintech, health, agriculture, and logistics that we are poised to unlock.

A Unique Ecosystem

Our model creates a support system that others cannot replicate, combining local grit with global expertise for maximum impact.

Impact and Return

We are committed to building businesses that not only generate financial returns but also change lives and create sustainable economic value.

Ready to Build the Future?

Whether you're an innovator with an idea, a partner who shares our vision, or an investor looking for impact, we want to hear from you.
